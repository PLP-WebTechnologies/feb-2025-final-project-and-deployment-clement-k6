Our Kenyan Story

Celebrating African heritage through fashion, craftsmanship, and community

From Nairobi to the World Our Journey

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Nairobi, AfroChic Kenya began as a small boutique with a big dream - to showcase the beauty of Kenyan craftsmanship to the world. What started as a passion project by founder Wanjiru Mwangi has grown into one of Kenya's most beloved fashion brands.

Our journey has been shaped by the vibrant colors, rich textures, and diverse cultures that make Kenya so special. Each piece in our collection tells a story of tradition meeting contemporary style.

Today, we're proud to support over 200 local artisans and craftspeople across Kenya, from Maasai beadworkers in Kajiado to textile weavers in Kisumu.

Our Core Values

Empowering Artisans

We provide fair wages and sustainable income opportunities for Kenyan craftspeople, preserving traditional skills for future generations.

Sustainable Practices

From organic cotton to natural dyes, we prioritize eco-friendly materials and processes that honor our environment.

Cultural Celebration

We design with purpose, blending traditional African aesthetics with modern silhouettes for global appeal.

Rooted in Kenyan Heritage

Kenya's diverse cultures inspire every collection we create. From the intricate beadwork of the Maasai to the bold Kitenge prints loved across East Africa, our designs pay homage to the artistry of Kenyan communities.

We partner directly with cooperatives and artisan groups across the country, ensuring authentic craftsmanship while creating economic opportunities in rural areas.
